what is mental health chatbot

A mental health chatbot is a tool that can provide information, support, and resources for people experiencing emotional challenges. It can't replace professional help, but it can be a safe space to chat and learn coping mechanisms.

Will it replace professional therapy

It's important to remember they are not a replacement for professional therapy, but rather a tool to support mental well-being.

what is the use for mental health chabot

Mental health chatbots offer 24/7 support and info, provide a safe space for anonymous conversation, and guide users through self-help tools.

how mental health chatbot will offer support and info

Mental health chatbots offer support and information by acting as a first point of contact. Through interactive questions, they assess a user's situation and provide personalized resources.
